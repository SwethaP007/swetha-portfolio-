About Swetha P Research Scholar

I am Swetha P, a dedicated Research Scholar on a scholarly journey driven by a deep enthusiasm for English Language Teaching. My research focuses on developing innovative, interdisciplinary strategies for effective English language acquisition, bridging the gap between traditional teaching methodologies and modern educational technologies.

My academic pursuits as a Research Scholar encompass English Literature Teaching, English as a Second Language (ESL) methodologies, and the integration of Artificial Intelligence in language learning environments. Through my research at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, I aim to create inclusive, dynamic learning environments that empower students to express themselves clearly and understand the nuances of language.

I am passionate about fostering connections with fellow educators, researchers, and professionals who share my commitment to advancing English language education and developing effective communication skills in diverse academic and professional contexts.
